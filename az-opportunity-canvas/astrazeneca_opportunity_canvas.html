02 · Canvas — Clinical Trial Decisioning
Canvas 01: Clinical Trial Decisioning Platform
The problem
Deciding which drug pursuits to advance, hold, or stop is the highest-stakes call in pharma — and it rests on more evidence than any team can hold in view: trial readouts, scientific literature, real-world data, competitive and regulatory signals. ML models score molecules and endpoints but can't reason over unstructured evidence or explain themselves, and portfolio prioritisation happens periodically rather than continuously. The result is slower decisions, late-stage failures, and capital committed to the wrong programmes.
Stakeholder landscape
Chief Medical Officer / R&D leadershipOwn pipeline decisions and the cost of getting them wrong
Heads of Clinical DevelopmentRun the trials that generate the evidence to act on
Portfolio & Pipeline StrategyPrioritise across programmes under finite capital
Biostatistics & Data ScienceOwn the ML models the platform works alongside

03 · Canvas — Engineering Digital Twin
Canvas 02: Engineering Digital Twin Agents
The problem
The physical engineering behind manufacturing — facility builds, process scale-up, production lines — is capital-intensive, highly regulated (GMP), and unforgiving when it goes wrong. Digital twins of these processes exist, but they are largely passive: they model, they don't govern. Scenario testing and quality control are manual and periodic, so problems surface late and corrections are expensive. The opportunity is agents that act around the twin — simulating scenarios, enforcing control, and catching quality issues before they reach the physical process.
Stakeholder landscape
VP Engineering / ManufacturingOwns delivery of facilities, scale-up and production capacity
Operational Excellence leadershipAccountable for robustness, throughput and cost of failure
Quality (QA / QC) leadershipOwns quality control and the GMP standard the agents must uphold
Process & Automation EngineersEnd users — interact with the twin and the agents day to day
Unique Anthropic value
Agents that act around the twinRun scenario simulations, interpret results, and flag risk before it goes physical
Governance & control by designBounded behaviour with humans in the loop — won't take unsafe actions, escalates instead
Auditable decisionsEvery action explainable and logged — essential in a GMP environment
Unique Accenture value
Industrial & digital twin deliveryEngineering and manufacturing transformation depth at scale
OT & automation integrationConnecting agents to the twin, control systems and the shop floor
GMP-validated deploymentQuality-systems expertise to deploy into regulated manufacturing

04 · Canvas — MES Chatbot
Canvas 03: MES Chatbot for GMBR Review
The problem
PAS-X MBR configurations are stored as large, deeply nested XML files. Across design, validation and review cycles, teams spend significant manual effort locating configuration elements, identifying hardcoded values, verifying compliance against Körber guidance, and cross-checking consistency across GMBRs. This sits inside a GxP-regulated environment, so a wrong answer is worse than no answer — and the work is dependent on scarce specialist knowledge of both the XML schema and the PAS-X application. The opportunity is a chatbot that reads the XML accurately, cites every answer at step level, and never fabricates a value that isn't in the file — turning hours of manual inspection into a plain-English conversation.
Stakeholder landscape
Digital Manufacturing LeadOwns the AI initiative and sponsors adoption across the Axial program
Process SMEsVerify structural compliance against Körber guidance and internal standards
Quality TeamsCross-check configuration consistency across multiple GMBRs for review
Validation EngineersInterrogate MBR configuration data daily — the chatbot's primary user
Unique Anthropic value
Never fabricates, never assumesReports blanks as blank and never invents a step ID, value or count not in the file
Cites every answerEvery response ties to a step customId and its position in the GMBR hierarchy
Formula assistantReads, debugs and suggests PAS-X formulas in valid curly-bracket syntax — with clear SME boundaries
Unique Accenture value
Körber compliance benchmarkingChecks a GMBR against Körber's complexity and loop limits, flagging cross-product risk
Cross-GMBR comparisonCompares configuration patterns across multiple GMBRs to surface inconsistencies
Axial program delivery contextEmbedded in the live 10+ site MES rollout, not a standalone pilot
